Hai Phong is a major tourism center of Vietnam. As a big city near the sea and islands, Hai Phong is an important part in the robust Hanoi – Hai Phong – Quang Ninh economic triangle.
Hai Phong has many world-class tourist centres of high quality such as 4 stars resorts and casinos, Do Son golf, the largest filtered seawater pool with artificial waves in Asia, hot spring resorts in Tien Lang, Cat Ba island and Cat Ba resort and Spa in Cat Ba island, etc.

Haiphong cuisine is one of the cuisines which follow the processing style of using seafood as the material platform.
Some dishes originated in Haiphong like field crab red noodle soup, crab spring rolls, field crab hot pot, sautéed snail, Hai Phong spicy sandwich (also called sandwich stick) , unique and delicious!!!
